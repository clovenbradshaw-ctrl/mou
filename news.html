The Proposed MOU

The resolution is a proposed memorandum of understanding between Metro and the Nashville Downtown Partnership (NDP), a private nonprofit composed of downtown property owners and businesses. The MOU outlines how the NDP would use the $15 million grant announced in October.

"Under this MOU, NDP agrees to limit the uses of the funds to a finite list, all of which are already permitted by Metro. Absent the MOU, NDP has the option to apply the funds to a wider list of uses, though they've stated they will not do so." ‚Äî Julie Oaks Smith, Mayor's Office Communications Director

Councilmember Erin Evans, former chair of the Public Health and Safety Committee, told the Banner she anticipated many members will seek to defer the resolution. The administration has said it will not oppose those efforts.
